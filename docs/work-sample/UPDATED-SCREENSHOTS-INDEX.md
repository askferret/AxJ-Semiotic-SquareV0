## Sample Selection Rationale for EPIC

The **AI Adoption in Organizations** sample was added to the demo data specifically for EPIC 2026 because:

1. **Organizational focus:** Maps workplace tensions (automation vs. expertise, displacement vs. augmentation)
2. **Ethnographic relevance:** Captures multiple stakeholder perspectives (C-suite, workers, managers, displaced employees)
3. **Current urgency:** AI adoption in industry is a live research and policy domain
4. **Methodological clarity:** Shows how the tool helps researchers map non-binary positions in organizational change
5. **Audience alignment:** More relevant to EPIC participants (anthropologists, ethnographers in industry) than journalism-only examples
